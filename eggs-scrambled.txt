Scrambled Eggs:
1. Get some eggs.
2. Get a frying pan
3. Put bacon greese in pan over low-medium heat
4. Crack eggs into bowl.
6. Beat eggs with a fork unless yoke and white is well blended.
7. Pour beaten eggs into pan.
8. Stir eggs using flipper or large spoon.
9. When eggs are no longer runny empty pan onto a plate.
10. Salt and peper to taste.
11. Enjoy.
